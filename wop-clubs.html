World Of Pool Billiards (WOP)

About the clubs.

World Of Pool Billiards (WOP)is a club dedicated to all those who are passionate about the sport of Pool Billiards. It is strategically located in the heart of District 10, between Saigon's bustling center, and boasts a large-scale facility with 31 pool tables and 2 VIP rooms - truly living up to its name, "The World of Pool Billiards." Operating hours are from 8:30 AM to 5:00 AM every day.

Number of pool tables
31 pool tables
VIP rooms
2 rooms
When you visit WOP, our valued customers, you will experience the utmost comfort, a welcoming atmosphere, and joyful ambiance, along with professional services ranging from attentive customer care to delicious food and beverages. Our staff members are quick and agile, always aiming to create a friendly and lively environment for our customers.
Furthermore, with our mission to develop and elevate the sport of pool in Ho Chi Minh City and Vietnam as a whole, we go beyond providing a mere playing field. We also offer training and learning opportunities through courses in collaboration with the Ho Chi Minh City Billiards & Snooker Association.
